I am a postdoc at the Institute of Aerodynamics and Gas Dynamics of the University of Stuttgart. I obtained my PhD in the same department, analyzing civil aircraft in low speed stall.

I work in aircraft aerodynamics, focusing mainly on the boundaries of the envelope. My projects are mostly on the computational side of things, mainly hybrid RANS/LES of wake flows. My research revolves around data-driven analysis of turbulent flows and I try to find out how to make sense of large scale separated flows.
